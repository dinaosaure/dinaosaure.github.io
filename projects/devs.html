Devs (2023)

A rogue-like video game developed with Godot and C#.

Procedural dungeons, turn-based combat, and custom sprites.

Devs is a rogue-like video game created as part of the 1st year programming curriculum at EPITA. The project involved procedural level generation, multiple character classes, and a variety of enemies and items. It was an opportunity to learn about game development, teamwork, and the Godot engine.

Source Code

Stack & Tools

  • Languages: C#
  • Engine: Godot
  • Tools: Git, GitHub
  • Platform: Windows, Linux

Key Features

  • Turn-Based Combat: Strategic gameplay with multiple character classes.
  • Tutorial & Multiple Levels: The game features an introductory tutorial and several distinct levels to explore and master.
  • Custom Sprites & Animations: Visuals mixing original pixel art and open-source assets.
  • Team-Based Development: Collaboration using Git and GitHub.

Game Design & Inspirations

  • Rogue-like Inspiration: The game draws from classic and modern rogue-likes, focusing on replayability, challenge, and the thrill of starting over to surpass your previous runs. The permadeath mechanic and randomization keep each playthrough engaging.
  • Cyberpunk Universe: The world of Devs is set in a dystopian, cyberpunk future, inspired by works like Cyberpunk 2077 and Edgerunners. The contrast between advanced technology and social decay shapes the game‚Äôs atmosphere and visual identity.
  • Gameplay & Progression: Strategic, turn-based combat encourages thoughtful play. Players unlock new skills and upgrades as they progress, with a dynamic experience and skill system. The game features a currency system, shop, and a variety of enemies with unique AI behaviors.
  • Art & Audio: Custom visuals and pixel art assets, mixing original creations and open-source resources, give the game a distinctive, retro-futuristic look. The soundtrack, inspired by synthwave, enhances immersion and nostalgia.
  • Player Experience: Special attention was given to UI/UX, menus, and feedback (pause, game over, sound settings, etc.) to ensure a smooth and engaging experience for all players.
  • Technical & Creative Challenges: The project was a first for the team in game development, requiring mastery of Godot, C#, version control, and collaborative workflows. Implementing multiplayer, AI, and level design were major learning opportunities.
  • Vision: The ultimate goal was to create a fun, authentic, and accessible rogue-like that would entertain and challenge players, while serving as a springboard for future creative projects.
