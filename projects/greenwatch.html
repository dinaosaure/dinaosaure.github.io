GreenWatch - Environmental Monitoring App

As part of my international semester in Madrid, where I completed the second part of my second year at EPITA, I had the opportunity to take a course at Universidad PolitÃ©cnica de Madrid (UPM). This project, GreenWatch, was developed during that semester and represents a practical application of the skills acquired through immersive learning in mobile development and team collaboration using Flutter.

Working in a team of three, we developed GreenWatch: a mobile application that helps users monitor and be informed about the environmental impacts of droughts and floods. It provides real-time data and predictive analytics to assist individuals, communities, and organizations in managing water-related natural disasters.

One of the main challenges was integrating a wide range of complex and interconnected functionalities into a user-friendly Flutter app. We adopted a methodical approach, structuring the app architecture early and iterating regularly. Through extensive documentation research and technical exploration, we deepened our understanding of the Flutter ecosystem. Git and GitHub were essential for our collaboration and versioning.

This project allowed us to strengthen our mobile development skills, particularly in areas such as state management, third-party integrations (flutter_map, Firebase, sqflite), and responsive UI design. We also gained valuable experience in problem-solving, debugging, and working under project constraints and deadlines.
