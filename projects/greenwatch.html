GreenWatch (2024)

Your ally for monitoring and staying informed about the environmental impacts of droughts and floods.

Stay informed, stay prepared.

GreenWatch is a cutting-edge Flutter-based mobile application designed to help users monitor and stay informed about the environmental impacts of droughts and floods. With real-time data and predictive analytics, the app empowers individuals, communities, and organizations to stay one step ahead when it comes to managing water-related natural disasters. Whether you’re looking to track ongoing drought conditions or monitor flood risks, GreenWatch provides vital information and updates, ensuring you are always prepared for the unexpected.

Source Code Project Website

Stack & Tools

  • Languages: Dart (Flutter)
  • Libraries: flutter_map, Firebase, sqflite
  • Tools: Git, GitHub, Figma
  • Platforms: Android, iOS

Key Features

  • Real-Time Data: Uses sensors, satellite imagery, and local weather data for up-to-date insights.
  • Predictive Analytics: Provides predictions and alerts regarding drought and flood events.
  • Community Reports: Integrates community-driven reports and observations for a comprehensive picture.
  • Interactive Map: OpenStreetMap integration, custom event markers, dynamic route drawing.
  • User Authentication: Secure login (Email/Password, Google, Guest mode).
  • Local Data Persistence: Store and manage events locally (CRUD, SQFlite).
  • Customizable UI: Light/Dark mode, user preferences, modern responsive design.
  • RESTful API Integration: Fetch and display external data (weather, environmental APIs).

Environmental Impact & Justification

  • GreenWatch is a proactive environmental monitoring solution that aligns with global efforts to mitigate the effects of climate change.
  • By providing real-time insights into drought and flood risks, the app promotes better resource management and early warning systems that can save lives and minimize damage.
  • Raises awareness about water scarcity, extreme weather events, and the importance of sustainable water practices.
  • Empowers individuals and communities to take informed actions and fosters a more resilient future.
