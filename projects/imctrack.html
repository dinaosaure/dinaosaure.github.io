IMCTrack (2025)

Android mobile application for BMI tracking and health monitoring.

Track your health journey with precision.

IMCTrack is a comprehensive Android mobile application developed in Kotlin during my international semester at UPM. The app allows users to track their BMI (Body Mass Index) over time, providing features for data entry, visualization, and analysis. This project was an excellent opportunity to deepen my skills in Android development, UI/UX design, and mobile app architecture while focusing on health and wellness technology.

Source Code

Stack & Tools

  • Languages: Kotlin
  • Platform: Android
  • Tools: Android Studio, Git, GitHub
  • Libraries: Android SDK, Material Design Components

Key Features

  • User Authentication: Secure login and user profile management.
  • Data Storage: Local data persistence for BMI records and user information.
  • Interactive Charts: Visual representation of BMI trends over time.
  • BMI Calculator: Automatic BMI calculation based on height and weight inputs.
  • Progress Tracking: Historical data analysis and progress monitoring.
  • Modern UI/UX: Material Design implementation with responsive layouts.
  • Notifications: Reminders and alerts for health tracking.

Health & Wellness Focus

  • Health Technology: IMCTrack represents the intersection of mobile technology and health monitoring, contributing to the growing field of digital health solutions.
  • User Empowerment: By providing easy-to-use tools for health tracking, the app empowers users to take control of their health journey and make informed decisions.
  • Data Privacy: The app prioritizes user privacy and data security, ensuring personal health information remains confidential and protected.
  • Accessibility: Designed with accessibility in mind, making health tracking available to users of all technical abilities.
